Ethan R. Elenberg




About



I am a Ph. D. Candidate at the University of Texas at Austin under the joint supervision of Prof. Sriram Vishwanath and Prof. Alex Dimakis.

I completed my B.E. in Electrical Engineering from The Cooper Union for the Advancement of Science and Art in 2012 and received my M.S in Electrical and Computer Engineering from UT Austin in 2014. I am a member of the Wireless Networking and Communications Group (WNCG), as well as the Laboratory for Informatics, Networks and Communications (LINC).
